Instructional Management

Supervisor Guide

Role Overview

Supervisors play a critical role in the student journey, providing direct oversight and support within specific training cohorts. This guide details the tools available for monitoring progress, providing technical assistance, and generating performance reports.

The Supervisor Portal

The portal serves as a real-time monitor for cohort performance. Supervisors can track attendance, topic completion, and exam results to identify students requiring additional intervention.

Cohort Monitoring

Comprehensive visibility into student progress is maintained through the cohort dashboard. Visibility is segmented by program (DIP or WRP) to ensure structured oversight.

Real-Time Completion: Metrics are updated as students complete modules, allowing for immediate identification of average progress across the group.
Individual Tracking: Expanding a cohort card reveals the detailed status of each student, including their last active timestamp and exam performance.

Student Support & Recovery

In the event of credential loss or technical blockage, supervisors have the authority to trigger recovery workflows.

Password Recovery: The refresh utility next to a student's record generates a new temporary password, enabling immediate platform re-entry for the learner.

Reporting & Documentation

Formal student data can be exported at any time to facilitate external reporting and administration.

The "Export to Excel" utility generates a multi-sheet report including:

  • Attendance and Progress percentages per student.
  • Module-by-module completion status.
  • Consolidated cohort summaries and pass rates.

Managing Learning Groups

Supervisors can establish and maintain learning environments through the cohort management utility. Each new cohort generates a unique invite link and identification code, which are distributed to learners for self-registration.
