# Spot the Mistake

You've covered verbal communication, written communication, and interview readiness. Now let's test how sharp your eye is.

**How to play:**
- Each round, one player is in the hot seat
- You have **60 seconds** to spot as many professional mistakes as possible
- Choose a scenario: Interview Attire, Professional Email, or Interview Behaviour
- The player who finds the most mistakes wins

This is a competitive round — everyone plays, scores are tracked, and the leaderboard reveals the winner.
