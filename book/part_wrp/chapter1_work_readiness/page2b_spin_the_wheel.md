# Elevator Pitch Game

You've just learned how to craft a 60-second elevator pitch. Now it's time to deliver one — live, under pressure, in front of the group.

The wheel will randomly select who goes next. When your name lands, you have **60 seconds** to deliver your best elevator pitch.

**Remind yourself of the structure:**

- **Who you are** — your name and background
- **What you do / what you're learning** — your skills and program
- **What you want** — the opportunity or role you're targeting
- **Why them** — one specific reason you're excited about this

Ready? Spin the wheel.
