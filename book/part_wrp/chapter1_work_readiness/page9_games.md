# Interactive Games & Activities

Learning sticks when it is fun. These games are designed to reinforce everything you have covered in this program — through competition, laughter, and a little pressure.

---

## Game 1 — Spot the Mistake

You have 60 seconds. Find as many professional mistakes as you can. The player who finds the most wins. Names appear on the leaderboard in real time.

<spot-the-mistake />

---

## Game 2 — Spin the Wheel

Add everyone's name to the wheel. Spin it. Whoever it lands on has 60 seconds to deliver their elevator pitch to the group. No preparation — just go.

<spin-the-wheel />

---

## Game 3 — Buzz Word Bingo

During the next mock interview or group discussion, mark off the professional buzzwords as you hear them. First to complete a row shouts "Bingo!" and wins.

<buzzword-bingo />
