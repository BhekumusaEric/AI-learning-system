# Speed Networking

Great professionals don't just know their craft — they know *people*. Networking is one of the most underrated career skills, and today you get to practise it live.

**In this activity you will:**
- Join the live room with your class code
- See who else is online right now
- Send a peer invite to someone you'd like to chat with
- Once accepted, you'll enter a private 1-on-1 chat to:
  - Introduce yourself
  - Practice your elevator pitch on each other
  - Give each other feedback on your CVs or answers

> 💡 **Tip:** Don't just say "Hi." Jump straight into your 60-second pitch as if you'd just met at a career fair!
