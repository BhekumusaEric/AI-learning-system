## Breathing Exercise — 4-7-8 Technique — 4-7-8 Technique

This takes **60 seconds**. Do it now before continuing.

1. **Inhale** through your nose for **4 counts**
2. **Hold** your breath for **7 counts**
3. **Exhale** slowly through your mouth for **8 counts**
4. Repeat **3 times**

This technique activates your parasympathetic nervous system — it physically reduces anxiety within seconds. Athletes, CEOs, and public speakers use it before high-pressure moments.

| Skill | What it means |
|-------|--------------|

Studies show that **85% of jobs are filled through networking and personal connections** — not just applications. This means how you present yourself, communicate, and build relationships matters enormously.

Your skills got you in the room. Your communication skills will get you the job.
