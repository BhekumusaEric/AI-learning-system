# Roast My Pitch 🔥

Think you've nailed your elevator pitch or cover letter? Time to find out! 

One brave volunteer submits their pitch to the room. Everyone else gets to drop **anonymous sticky notes** directly onto the text — highlighting what works, what's weak, and what's missing.

**Rules of engagement:**
- Be **constructive**, not cruel — real feedback, not roasting for the sake of it
- Point out **specific** strengths with 🟢 green notes
- Flag specific weaknesses with 🔴 red notes
- Focus on **clarity**, **confidence**, and **professionalism**

> 💡 Getting honest peer feedback before a real interview is one of the most valuable things you can do. Be brave enough to go first!
