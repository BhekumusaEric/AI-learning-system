# What Would You Do?

The workplace is full of grey areas. Read the scenario below and vote live with your group on what you'd do. There's no single right answer — the point is to think it through and **discuss**.

Your facilitator will guide the conversation and advance the story based on how the room votes.

> 💡 **Remember:** In professional settings, how you handle difficult situations says more about you than your CV ever could.
