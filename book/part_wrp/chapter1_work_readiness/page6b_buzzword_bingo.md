# Buzzword Bingo

You've just studied LinkedIn profiles and personal branding. One thing you'll notice quickly in the professional world — people love buzzwords.

**How to play:**
- Everyone gets a unique shuffled bingo card
- As your facilitator reads out LinkedIn profiles or job descriptions, mark off every buzzword you hear
- First to complete a row, column, or diagonal shouts **BINGO!**

This game trains you to recognise empty corporate language — and to replace it with specific, evidence-based language in your own profile.
