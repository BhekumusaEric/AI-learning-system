# CV & Cover Letter Builder

Build your ATS-friendly CV and cover letter directly in this platform. Fill in your details, preview the result, then download as PDF or email it to yourself.

## What is ATS?

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) are software tools used by most companies to automatically scan and filter CVs before a human ever reads them. An ATS-friendly CV uses:

- Clean formatting with no tables, columns, or graphics
- Standard section headings (Experience, Education, Skills)
- Keywords from the job description
- A single-column layout that parses correctly

> Studies show that **75% of CVs are rejected by ATS before reaching a recruiter.** A well-structured CV gets you past the filter.

## The Two Documents You Need

Every job application requires two documents:

| Document | Purpose | Length |
|----------|---------|--------|
| **CV / Resume** | Your professional history and skills | 1–2 pages |
| **Cover Letter** | Why you want this specific role at this specific company | 1 page |

A CV without a cover letter is a missed opportunity. A cover letter shows initiative, communication skills, and genuine interest.
